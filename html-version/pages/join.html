Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join?

Any student enrolled at Oriental College of Technology can join, regardless of department or year.

Is there a membership fee?

No, membership is completely free. However, some specialized workshops may have nominal charges for materials.

Do I need prior AI/ML knowledge?

No! We welcome beginners. We provide foundational workshops and mentorship for students at all levels.

What is the time commitment?

It's flexible! Attend events that interest you. Active members typically spend 4-6 hours per week.

Can I join multiple teams?

Yes! While you'll have a primary team, you can contribute to other teams based on your interests.

When will I hear back?

You'll receive a confirmation email within 2-3 working days after submitting your application.
